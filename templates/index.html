Change your home with an Extension.

An extension is a simple and popular way of adding additional space to your home. When we build a house extension, kitchen extension or bedroom extension we always ensure that there will be minimum disruption to you and your home. Wherever possible, we will separate the living area from the building work for as long as we can and will complete the project within the agreed timeframe.

Concerned with approval? Not all extensions require Planning Approval, therefore you may be able to build your extension under a Certificate of Lawfulness.
